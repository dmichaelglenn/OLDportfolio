As a developer, I'm quickly finding myself drawn to minimalist practices and aesthetics. I see a great deal of beauty in simplicity, and I'm always moved by the artistry exibited in creating an elegant and functional web app using minimal resources, therby ensuring the smoothest experience for the greatest number of users. However, I've been known to go over the top when an occasion calls for it...programming is a (very fun) art form, and I try not to take myself too seriously.
